# MePlayer – Modern, Open‑Source Music Player for Android

**MePlayer** is a simple, lightweight, and privacy‑focused music player built entirely with **Kotlin** and **Jetpack Compose**. It follows **Material Design 3** (Material You) guidelines and provides a seamless listening experience – completely free of ads and tracking.

---

## ✨ Key Features
- **🎨 Material You** – Dynamic theming that adapts to your wallpaper.
- **🔍 Privacy First** – No analytics, no trackers, no unnecessary permissions.
- **🎵 Local Playback** – Plays all audio files stored on your device (MP3, FLAC, AAC, etc.) using **ExoPlayer**.
- **🌙 Dark & Light Themes** – Automatic or manual theme switching.
- **🔔 Notification Controls** – Playback controls in the status bar.
- **💾 Lightweight** – Small APK size and minimal memory usage.

---

## 🛡️ Permissions Explained
MePlayer requests only the permissions it truly needs, and all sensitive permissions are requested at runtime with clear explanations:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `READ_MEDIA_AUDIO` (Android 13+) / `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` (≤ Android 12) | Read audio files from your device storage. |
| `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` (≤ Android 9) | Delete audio files when you choose to remove them. On Android 10+ deletion uses the system dialog (`MediaStore.createDeleteRequest`) for extra safety. |
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` (Android 13+) | Show media playback notification. You can disable it in settings. |
| `FOREGROUND_SERVICE` + `FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK` | Keep playback alive in the background (required by Android policy). |

No data is collected or shared – your music stays on your device.
